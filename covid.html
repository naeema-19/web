WHAT IS COVID 19 AND CORONA VIRUS?

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus. In humans, the virus causes respiratory infections which are typically mild but, in rare cases, can be lethal.

SYMPTOMS

  • Cough
  • Fever
  • Shortness of breath
  • In some cases loss of taste

HOW TO PROTECT YOURSELF AND OTHERS?

  • WEAR MASK :cover the nose and mouth
  • KEEP SOCIAL DISTANCING :avoid crowd especially in poorly ventilated rooms
  • WASH YOUR HANDS more often
  • FOLLOW THE COVID PROTOCOLS LAID DOWN BY THE GOVERNMENT
